Give Some for the Ones Who Gave All

Trips 4 Taps offers free flights and lodging for Veterans wishing to attend the funerals of their fallen service members. We do this to ensure the men and women willing to pay the ultimate price for our freedom receive the honors they deserve in the event of their death. We give some for the ones who give all. Remember the lost by supporting Trips 4 Taps.
