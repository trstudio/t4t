Our Story

Trips 4 Taps started after the passing of Corporal Kyle Grady Breech, a US Marine who passed away in March of 2014. After hearing of his passing, former Marine Corporal William Jones organized 14 Marines to attend his funeral on a two days notice.

The funeral gave Corporal Jones an opportunity to re-connect with his fellow Marines, who had all lost touch after finishing their enlistment, and served as a way for the Marines present to heal and bond over their loss. As a result of the experience, Corporal Jones decided to offer this opportunity to any veteran in the same situation.
